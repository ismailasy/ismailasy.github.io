Research Interests

In our increasingly interconnected world, where the digital realm intertwines seamlessly with our everyday lives, the significance of safeguarding sensitive information has reached new heights. My research interests span a wide spectrum of critical domains at this intersection of technology and security. These areas not only signify the transformative potential of modern technology but also underscore the pressing necessity of comprehensive security measures to protect our digital assets. Among my primary concerns is the imperative to ensure the utmost security for medical data, given its inherent sensitivity and the profound impact it holds on individuals' well-being and privacy.

My research focuses on :

  • Internet of Things (IoT): The IoT represents a dynamic landscape of interconnected devices and sensors, promising to revolutionize how we interact with technology. However, ensuring the security and privacy of IoT data is paramount, as vulnerabilities in this ecosystem can have far-reaching consequences.
  • Wireless Networks: The proliferation of wireless communication technologies has opened up new possibilities for connectivity, but it has also introduced security challenges. My research delves into the vulnerabilities of wireless networks and strategies to protect against cyber threats.
  • Next-Generation Networks: Next-gen networks hold the key to enhanced performance, scalability, and innovation. Yet, as we move toward these advanced architectures, it is crucial to address security concerns to ensure the integrity and reliability of these networks.
  • Steganography: Steganography is the art of concealing information within digital media, such as images or audio files. This field explores the covert communication techniques that can be harnessed for secure data transmission and privacy protection.
  • Medical Data Security: Within the realm of healthcare, the protection of sensitive medical data is of paramount importance. My research focuses on developing robust security measures to safeguard this data, ensuring it remains confidential and unaltered, all while maintaining accessibility for authorized entities.
  • Blockchain: Blockchain technology is reshaping industries with its decentralized and tamper-resistant nature. My research in this area focuses on the security and privacy aspects of blockchain applications, exploring how this technology can be leveraged for secure transactions and data management.
